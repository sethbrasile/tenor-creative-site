# Messaging & Copy Frameworks

**Project:** Tenor Creative Redesign
**Researched:** 2026-01-25
**Domain:** Digital Agency for SMBs (Web, SEO, CRM)

## Recommended Frameworks

### Primary Framework: The "Guide" Model (StoryBrand adapted)
**Why:** Small business owners are overwhelmed. They don't need a hero (you); they need a guide.
**Structure:**
1.  **The Character:** SMB Owner who wants growth but lacks time/expertise.
2.  **The Problem:** "I know I need digital marketing, but it's too complex/expensive."
3.  **The Guide (Tenor):** Empathy ("We know it's hard") + Authority ("We've helped 50+ locals").
4.  **The Plan:** 3 simple steps (e.g., "Schedule Call -> We Build -> You Grow").
5.  **The Call to Action:** "Get Your Free Audit" / "Start Growing".
6.  **The Success:** A thriving business, phone ringing.
7.  **The Failure:** Stagnation, losing to competitors.

### Secondary Pattern: PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution)
**Why:** High conversion for specific service pages (e.g., Local SEO).
**Structure:**
- **Problem:** "Is your business invisible on Google Maps?"
- **Agitation:** "Every day you aren't listed is a customer handing cash to your competitor."
- **Solution:** "Tenor's Local SEO gets you found in 30 days."

## Headline Formulas

| Formula | Example Application | Why It Works |
|---------|---------------------|--------------|
| **[Benefit] without [Pain]** | "Get a professional website without the agency price tag." | Addresses price sensitivity directly. |
| **The [Adjective] Way to [Result]** | "The stress-free way to manage your customer leads." | Promises simplicity/ease (key SMB value). |
| **[Action] your [Noun] in [Time]** | "Modernize your brand in just 2 weeks." | Specific timeline reduces anxiety of endless projects. |
| **We handle [Task], You handle [Business]** | "We handle the SEO, you handle the new customers." | Clarifies role division. |

## Value Propositions (The "Why")

**Core Value:** "Professional, Simple, Essential."

| Feature | Copy Translation (Good) | Copy Translation (Bad/Too Tech) |
|---------|-------------------------|---------------------------------|
| **Local SEO** | "Get found by neighbors looking for you." | "SERP optimization and schema markup." |
| **CRM** | "Never lose a customer lead again." | "Integrated pipeline management database." |
| **Web Design** | "A 24/7 salesperson that looks great." | "Responsive React-based frontend." |
| **Maintenance** | "We fix it before it breaks." | "99.9% uptime SLA and patch management." |

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

**Do:**
- **Professional but Accessible:** "We take this seriously, but we speak your language."
- **Direct:** Short sentences. Active verbs.
- **Empathetic:** Acknowledge their stress/busyness.

**Don't:**
- **Tech Jargon:** No "React", "Next.js", "Vercel", "Latency". Use "Fast", "Mobile-friendly".
- **Vague Hype:** Avoid "Synergy", "Transformation", "Disruptive".
- **Agency-Centric:** Minimize "We", maximize "You".

## Key Phrases

**Use These:**
- "Turnkey system"
- "Done for you"
- "Essential tools"
- "Local customers"
- "Simple monthly fee"

**Avoid These:**
- "Full-stack"
- "Bespoke solutions" (implies expensive)
- "Tech partner" (implies work for them)
- "Omnichannel"
