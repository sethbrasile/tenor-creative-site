# Phase 02: Core Experience - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-01-25
**Status:** Ready for planning

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

Implementation of the primary digital storefront through the Home, About, and Service pages (Web Design, Local SEO, CRM/GHL). This phase delivers the core marketing narrative and establishes the upsell bridge to Prickly Pear.

</domain>

<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Home Page Narrative
- **Primary Driver:** StoryBrand framework (Problem -> Solution -> Success).
- **Information Density:** Minimalist & Modern (high polish, ample white space).
- **Secondary Goal:** Preview Pricing Tiers to drive users toward the "Packages" page.
- **Social Proof:** Explicitly excluded for this phase. A plan to curate and add testimonials will be deferred to a later task.

### Service Page Consistency
- **Layout Style:** Flexible Template. Use a shared structural base for Hero and CTA sections, but allow custom middle sections to suit specific service needs.
- **Visual Identity:** Implement service-specific color accents (e.g., distinct shades for Web vs. SEO vs. CRM) to provide subtle visual cues.
- **Content Strategy:** Prioritize Business Outcomes (e.g., "Get more calls") over technical deliverables.
- **Primary Media:** Lead with Illustrations and Icons rather than photography or UI screenshots.

### Upsell Bridge (Prickly Pear)
- **Placement:** Global "Need More?" section positioned immediately above the footer on all major pages.
- **Visual Style:** Branded "Pop-out" section. It should be visually distinct from the Tenor brand to signify the transition to the parent agency.
- **Messaging:** Service-Gap focused. Explicitly mention services Tenor doesn't provide (e.g., Paid Ads, Video Production).
- **Interaction:** Direct external link to pricklypear.marketing.

### Interactivity & Motion
- **Animations:** Subtle fade and slide animations for page navigation, mouseovers, and clicks.
- **Scroll Behavior:** NO scroll-triggered animations. Maintain native browser scroll behavior without interference.
- **Navigation:** Smooth slide transition is allowed only when navigating to page anchors.
- **UI Patterns:** Use Accordion FAQs for Service pages and subtle interactive states for all hoverable elements (cards, buttons).

### Claude's Discretion
- Exact typography choices and spacing scales.
- Selection of specific icons/illustrations that fit the "Minimalist & Modern" goal.
- Implementation of the "Flexible Template" components.

</decisions>

<specifics>

- "Digital Storefront" should be the central theme of the Home Page.
- The Prickly Pear bridge should feel like a "Premium Upgrade" path.

</specifics>
